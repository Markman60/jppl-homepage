About JPPL

JPPL was founded to elevate pickleball in Japan to a professional level. We aim to unite players, fans, and sponsors through a world-class competitive experience.

JPPLの目的は、日本のピックルボールをプロスポーツへと進化させることです。

League Structure

  • Pro Divisions (Men’s / Women’s / Mixed)
  • City-based teams
  • Spring / Summer / Fall tournaments
  • Prize pools and rankings
